Q: What is the name of the character with the most script lines?
SQL: 
SELECT T1.name
FROM characters AS T1
INNER JOIN script_lines AS T2
  ON T1.id = T2.character_id
GROUP BY
  T1.id
ORDER BY
  COUNT(T2.id) DESC
LIMIT 1;

Q: Who are the children of Homer?
SQL: 
SELECT T1.name
FROM characters AS T1
JOIN characters AS T2
  ON T1.name LIKE '%' || SUBSTR(T2.name, INSTR(T2.name, ' ') + 1)
WHERE
  T2.name = 'Homer Simpson' AND T1.name <> 'Homer Simpson';

Q: How many lines of script does Homer Simpson have in Season 12, episode 10?
SQL: 
SELECT
  COUNT(T1.id)
FROM script_lines AS T1
INNER JOIN characters AS T2
  ON T1.character_id = T2.id
INNER JOIN episodes AS T3
  ON T1.episode_id = T3.id
WHERE
  T2.name = 'Homer Simpson' AND T3.season = 12 AND T3.number_in_season = 10;

Result: [(53,)]
